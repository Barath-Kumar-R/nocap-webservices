What We Do

Five disciplines, one unhurried standard.

Every treatment plan is built around a single consultation — no upsell script, no rushed chair time.

I.

Cosmetic Veneers

Hand-shaded porcelain veneers, planned from a full facial-aesthetic consult, not a catalogue shade.

II.

Dental Implants

Single-visit guided implant placement using 3D scans, for a restoration that looks and feels native.

III.

Invisible Aligners

Discreet alignment therapy with monthly in-studio reviews, no generic mail-order check-ins.

IV.

Root Canal Therapy

Microscope-assisted endodontics, completed in as few visits as the case genuinely allows.

V.

Preventive Studio Care

Twice-yearly reviews with a private hygienist, and a direct line to the doctor between visits.

VI.

Teeth Whitening

In-studio whitening calibrated to your natural shade — never bleached past what looks believable.
